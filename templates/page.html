To Apply

Send an email to hackersessions@cstuy.org with the subject "application." Include the following information in the email:

  • Your name.
  • Your email address.
  • Your parent's name and email address.
  • A contact phone number.
  • The grade in school you are in.
  • The name and email address of a teacher willing to write a brief recommendation for you.
  • A statement as to why you want to attend Hacking Sessions
  • Attach a scan or photo of your transcript or latest report card.
  • Attach a code sample (if you're applying as someone with experience, otherwise disregard this).
  • If you are applying for financial assistance, please indicate so.
